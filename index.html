Read my story

Hi, I’m Tihomir. I am a Web Developer with a background in CAD drafting and Entertainment. With CAD I got the chance to work on technical skills, time-management skills, and being detail-oriented. As a Hotel Entertainer in a big team, I was responsible for the organization and delivery of the daytime and evening entertainment program suited to all hotel guests. I worked on creativity, interpersonal skills, empathetic listening, conflict resolution, negotiation, improvisation. Now I would like to combine my love for technical skills and work in a team to create innovative Web applications that meet the needs of my users.

After learning basics in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript I decided to enroll in Fullstack Web Developer Bootcamp with CareerFoundry(duration 7 months). There I got the chance to learn about topics such as Server-side programming & Node.js, Client-side programming & React, Testing in the Development process, Native App Development & React Native, and a little bit of Angular. There is much more to learn and I look forward to it. I love to work in a team, and I am a great team player, interested?
